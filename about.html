About CodeMaster Institute

Empowering students with industry-relevant programming skills since 2015

Our Mission

To make quality programming education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background. We aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing practical, hands-on training.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted programming institute, recognized for producing skilled developers who drive innovation and technological advancement in the industry.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, and Student Success. We believe in continuous learning and adapting to the ever-evolving technology landscape.

Our Story

2015

Humble Beginnings

CodeMaster Institute started as a small coaching center with just 3 courses: C, C++, and Java. Our founder, Mr. Rajesh Kumar, identified the gap between academic curriculum and industry requirements.

2017

Expansion

We expanded our course offerings to include Python and Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). Our student base grew to 500+ learners, and we moved to a larger facility.

2019

Online Platform Launch

Launched our online learning platform to reach students across the country. Introduced PHP & MySQL course and started offering hybrid learning options.

2022

Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with tech companies for internships and placement opportunities. Crossed the milestone of 10,000+ students trained.

2024

Present Day

Offering 15+ comprehensive programming courses with 50+ expert instructors. Continuously updating curriculum to include latest technologies and industry trends.
