Course FAQs

Do I need prior programming experience?

Most beginner courses require no prior experience. For intermediate and advanced courses, we recommend completing prerequisites or having basic programming knowledge.

What is the class schedule?

We offer flexible batches: Morning (9 AM - 12 PM), Afternoon (2 PM - 5 PM), Evening (6 PM - 9 PM), and weekend batches. You can choose what works best for you.

Are courses available online?

Yes, all courses are available both online and offline. Online classes include live sessions, recorded lectures, and interactive doubt-solving.

What is the refund policy?

We offer a 7-day refund policy from the course start date. If you're not satisfied with the course, you can request a full refund within this period.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we provide complete placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, and connecting with our hiring partners.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including EMI and installment plans. Contact our admission office for details.
