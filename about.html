Our Story

From Classroom Teaching to Institute Founding

CodeMaster Institute was born out of a simple realization: while teaching programming at various educational institutions, I noticed that students often struggled to apply theoretical knowledge to practical problems. The curriculum focused heavily on theory but provided little hands-on experience with real-world coding.

After 4 years at GLD Public School Khairtal (CBSE), followed by 1 year at Govt. Laxmaneshwar College, and 3 years at Shri Mahant Laldas Art & Science College, I had taught hundreds of students. Many of them were bright and capable but lacked the practical skills that employers were looking for.

In 2020, I decided to create a different kind of learning environment - one that focuses on:

  • Practical Coding: More time writing code, less time just reading about it
  • Project-Based Learning: Building real applications from day one
  • Industry Relevance: Teaching technologies actually used in the workplace
  • Personal Attention: Small batches with individual mentoring

Today, CodeMaster Institute has helped numerous students transform their careers, with many now working as professional developers in top companies.
